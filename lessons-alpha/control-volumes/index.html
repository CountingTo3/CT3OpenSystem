Control Volume Analysis

ID: 00001   LM: 2025MAY24

    Control volume analysis is a thermodynamics topic regarding the change in energy of some specific volume. Unlike the experimental idea of "control", the volume can change depending on the conditions. In this case, "control" refers to the control of your choice of volume.
    According to conservation of energy, the following equation applies:

Ein = Eout

    It is important to note that sign conventions differ. The following is the sign convention I was taught:

Q = W + E

Qin - Win = Qout - Wout
